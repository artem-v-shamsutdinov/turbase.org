Turbase

Turbase is a revolutionary new App that keeps your data on your device.  It shares data with other devices through IPFS and Arweave blockchains.  Phone and web applications access data directly on the device without having to store it themselves or send it over the network.  Turbase puts you in control of who you share data with and how much you share.

Turbase uses the AIRport Decentralized Application framework.  It is now under ​heavy development and will be ​available in 2022.  Subscribe to the Turbase channel to stay tuned.

Turbase contains an ​Advertisement engine on each device.  It is independent of all other devices and eliminates centralized data warehouses.  Each Turbase App requests specific Ads using only the data on its device.  It never shares your data with any Ad provider.

Turbase splits the advertisement revenue between the used Apps and you.  When you use Turbase based Apps you get a cut of the profits.

Blockchain secured data sharing

Turbase splits all data into virtual repositories.  Each virtual repository is shared ​independently of others and maintains its own private blockchain.  ​This ensures data authenticity and allows for conflict resolution.  Private data is encrypted while public data stays open for use by everybody.

Virtual repository blockchains are themselves shared via ​IPFS (for temporal data) or Arweave (for permanent data) blockchains.
